# Milestone 8 – Stats, Charts & Replay Inspector

_Goal: rich post-game analysis and visual inspection of recorded games._

## Success Criteria

- [ ] Post-game screen shows gold, XP, territory, and army line/area charts for all players.
- [ ] Scrubbing the replay timeline updates the chart highlight to match the current turn.
- [ ] Chart data can be exported as a valid CSV file.

---

- **Gold over time** – line chart per player across all turns.
- **XP over time** – line chart showing progression and divergence between players.
- **Territory over time** – area/stacked chart of sector ownership each turn.
- **Army over time** – soldier count per player.
- Charts available on:
  - The post-game results screen (summary of the just-finished game).
  - Inside the replay viewer (scrubbing updates the chart highlight to match the current turn).
- Export chart data as CSV from the replay inspector.
